Rural Development

Maa Samaleswari Education & Welfare Trust(MSEWT) is a voluntary development organisation working for the upliftment of rural people especially women and children. MSEWT was incepted in the year 2005. MSEWT has got its statutory recognition in the year Govt of India.MSEWT spreads its activities in all spheres and its integrated approach helps the overall development of women, children and the society. All the programmes of MSEWT are people's programme and with their fullest participation at every stages. We at MSEWT strongly believe in the strength of people especially women and children.
